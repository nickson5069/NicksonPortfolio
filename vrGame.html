Ship Box is a 1st person experience for the VR player. After detecting a signal from the spaceship, the unknown spaceship's secret was revealed.

You will be playing the character name MAS who is the leader of the astronaut's squad. After entering the spaceship, you decided to separate the team to discover the different paths of the spaceship which will help them discover faster. The player will be placed at the entrance of the ship with space gear and tools to help them explore the spaceship.
